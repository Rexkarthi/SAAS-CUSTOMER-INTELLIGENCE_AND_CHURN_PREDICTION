// Strategic Insights
📋
Contract Type is King
Month-to-Month contracts drive 29.1% of model signal — the single most powerful predictor. Customers on M2M contracts are 3.2× more likely to churn. Migrate high-value M2M customers to annual contracts with incentives.
Action: Contract Migration Campaign →
💸
Pricing Pressure is Real
Monthly charge + total revenue together account for 23% of feature importance. 43% of churners explicitly left for a competitor's better offer. Segment-specific pricing review is the highest-ROI retention lever.
Action: Competitive Price Audit →
📡
Fiber Optic Risk Signal
Fiber Optic internet type appears as the 7th most important feature — these high-paying customers have elevated churn risk, likely due to competitive alternatives. Premium retention offers needed here.
Action: Fiber Customer NPS Survey →
👥
Referrals = Loyalty
Number of referrals is a top-8 feature — customers who refer others are significantly less likely to churn. Amplifying the referral program is both a growth and retention strategy simultaneously.
Action: Referral Loyalty Program →
🎯
Threshold Strategy
Model uses threshold 0.27 (not default 0.5) to maximize churn recall to 85%. This is intentional — the cost of missing a churner ($70/mo × 18mo = $1,264) far exceeds the cost of a false positive intervention ($50).
Action: Threshold Sensitivity Analysis →
🔮
Next Steps: SHAP + CLV
Deploy SHAP explainability for per-customer "why they'll churn" explanations. Combine with Customer Lifetime Value scoring to prioritize interventions on high-CLTV churners first for maximum revenue impact.
Action: SHAP Integration Roadmap →
